DIRECTIONS

Drive on the N1 from Cape Town towards Worchester. About 6km after you exit the Huguenot Tunnel, turn right onto the R101 towards Rawsonville.

Carry on straight until the Slanghoek turn off on your left. Drive straight for 4km then turn right onto Goudiniweg towards Goudini Spa

Drive straight past the entrance of Goiudini Spa and Badsberg cellar for 1.3km then turn left on the gravel road at the Skilpadfontein sign

Stay on this road for about 6km till you get to the Skilpadfontein gate.
